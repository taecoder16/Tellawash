FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions)

    Is there a warranty?

  • Yes there is a one year warranty

    • How does the app work? And how does it correspond with the Telewash?

  • The app is free and will be able to connect to the Telewash. It will allow the user to turn on/off the Telewash, control the settings of both the washer and dryer; has a timer to let the user track the progress of their clothes and will send the user notifications to let them know that their clothes are done.

    • Where do I buy it?

  • You can buy product on our website or at local retail stores (i.e: Lowes, Walmart, Target, HHGregg, and Home Depot)

    • Do I have to get the app to make it the Telewash work?

  • No, it's optional

    • Can it fit in small spaces?

  • Yes it can fit in small spaces.

    • Does the product come pre assembled?

  • The product will be delivered to the owner's address and a member of our team will come out and assemble your washer and dryer for free.
