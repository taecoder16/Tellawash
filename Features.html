OUR IDEA WILL SAVE TIME AND MONEY

  
 

   

 
   

 
   
     

HOW IT WORKS

-With our idea you will be able to wash and dry your clothes with a easy process. it works by transferring the wash clothes to the dryer through a tube by its self or from your control through the app.


  

-For the dryer the settings will include- time dry,quick dry,air fluff ,damp dry,and eco dry.-for the washing machine the settings will include- normal wash,heavy duty,wrinkle away,time wash,towels,and sanitary.


 

The app is free and you will be able to connect to the tellawash. it will allow the user to turn on/off the telewash and control the settings of the washer and dryer.it also has a timer to let the user track the progress of their clothes and will send the user notifications to let them know that thier clothes are done.
